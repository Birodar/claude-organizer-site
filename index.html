Questions

Does it work with my existing Claude account?

Yes. Install the extension, open claude.ai, and a small 📂 button appears. Nothing changes about how you use Claude.

Where is my data stored?

Only in your own browser (Chrome's local storage). Claude Organizer has no servers and never uploads your conversations.

How do payments work?

Payments are securely handled by Lemon Squeezy. After you buy Pro, you receive a license key by email that unlocks the premium features.

Can I cancel a subscription?

Anytime. Your Pro features stay active until the end of the paid period, then the extension returns to the free plan.
