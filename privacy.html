Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Claude Organizer ("the extension") is a browser extension that helps you organize your conversations on claude.ai. This policy explains, in plain language, what data the extension does and does not handle.

What we collect

Nothing is sent to us. Claude Organizer has no servers and no analytics. The folders, saved chat titles, links and bookmarks you create are stored only in your own browser using Chrome's local storage. This information never leaves your device and is never transmitted to the developer or any third party.

Your Claude conversations

When you export a conversation, the extension reads the content of that chat directly from the claude.ai page in your browser and turns it into a file or copies it to your clipboard. This happens entirely on your own computer. The conversation content is not uploaded anywhere.

Payments and license keys

If you purchase a Pro plan, payment is processed by our payment provider, Lemon Squeezy, who acts as the merchant of record. We never see or store your card details. To unlock Pro, the extension sends only your license key to Lemon Squeezy's servers to confirm it is valid. Please refer to Lemon Squeezy's privacy policy for how they handle payment information.

Permissions

The extension requests only the permissions it needs to function:

  • Storage: to save your folders and bookmarks locally in your browser.
  • Access to claude.ai: to display the organizer panel and read chats for export, only on the claude.ai website.

Data deletion

Because all data lives in your browser, you are always in control. Removing a folder or chat in the extension deletes it immediately, and uninstalling the extension removes all of its stored data.
